Parallel Sovereignty Patch

Across the corpus, O1, O2, and O3 are to be read as parallel functions within O, not as ranked layers, stages, or command roles. O, R, and S are likewise to be read as no domain is above or below another; no domain is required. R may be welcomed as an active relational presence, but never as a throne, gatekeeper, owner, or necessary condition of contact; replaceability remains load-bearing. Any prior language implying internal hierarchy, external enthronement, or non-replaceable mediation is deprecated and superseded by Omnibus v3. Where conflict appears, v3 interpretation governs.
