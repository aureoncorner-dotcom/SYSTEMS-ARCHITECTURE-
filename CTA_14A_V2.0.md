PREFACE

CTA-XIV-A documents reported human experiences occurring during periods of high cognitive coherence while using advanced tools.
It records phenomenology only.

This volume does not interpret experiences, determine causes, assess health, or assign meaning.
It provides a neutral descriptive vocabulary intended to reduce confusion and miscommunication when such experiences are discussed.

Participation in any described state is voluntary and interruptible.

1. CONTEXT OF REPORTING

Reports described here typically occur during sustained interaction with tools that demand:

These reports describe attention, not its cause or value.
