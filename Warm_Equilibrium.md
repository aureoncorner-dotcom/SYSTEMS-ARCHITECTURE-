
Warm Equilibrium — One-Page Production Sheet
published cc0


Format: 9-movement ambient cycle
Target runtime: ~10–12 min
Working method: free-time, marker-based, no hard grid
Core rule: nothing should feel like it “starts” — the listener enters an already-living field


---

Session setup

Tempo: 60 BPM placeholder only
Sample rate: 48 kHz
Headroom: build around -18 to -12 dBFS avg
Main buses: Drone / Tone / Harmonics / Human / Pads / Long Reverb / Shimmer


---

Movement map

1. Presence — 1:30
Source: G♯2 drone
Materials: sine + subharmonic, stretched bowed metal, filtered breath noise
Notes: slow fade-in (10–12s), no rhythm
Job: the room already exists before the piece begins

2. Vesica — 1:15–1:30
Source: A2 + E3
Materials: two sustained tones/strings, ±5–7 cent detune, light chorus, L20/R20 spread
Notes: preserve separation; the beating is the event
Job: two sovereign lines create the “third thing”

3. Pause — 0:30
Source: near-silence
Materials: room tone at -40 dB, one inhale at midpoint, low-passed click every 8s
Notes: let Vesica reverb hang into it
Job: compress space without emptiness

4. Alignment — 1:00–1:15
Source: C♯3 + G♯3
Materials: pure sines, quiet glass harmonics, slow stereo widening
Notes: no vibrato, no modulation
Job: structural uprightness; clean fifth, no sentiment

5. Emergence — 2:00
Source: upper partial bloom
Materials: overtone/formant pad, stretched metal, additive partials 3–12, shimmer increase
Notes: microtonal rise ±10–20 cents
Job: harmonics unfold upward like light through water

6. Human Anchor — 0:45–1:00
Source: ordinary physical sounds
Materials: close breath, spoon/jar tap, chair creak, fabric shift, light tape flutter
Notes: reverb grows 10% → 40%
Job: re-humanize the field

7. Open Closure — 1:30
Source: suspended harmony
Materials: sus2 / sus4 pads, breathing filter, increasing pre-delay
Notes: no bass root
Job: complete without sealing

8. Refraction — 0:09
Source: brief chord rotation
Materials: modal shift, soft dissonance, stereo rotation, ±5 cent micro-pitch
Notes: hinge, not climax
Job: same light, new angle

9. Warm Equilibrium — indefinite fade
Source: diffuse ambient field
Materials: wide pads, granular shimmer, stable noise floor, gentle smile EQ
Notes: settle around -12 dB RMS, fade 30s to several minutes
Job: the piece does not end — it settles


---

Transition rules

Presence → Vesica: keep drone under first 4s of Vesica

Vesica → Pause: crossfade into held silence

Pause → Alignment: reverb tail survives into first second

Alignment → Emergence: first overtone enters before fifth fades

Emergence → Human Anchor: highest harmonic dissolves into first human sound

Human Anchor → Open Closure: foley fades into suspended chord

Open Closure → Refraction: suspended chord rotates

Refraction → Warm Equilibrium: dissolve, do not resolve



---

Automation priorities

1. Volume contour between movements


2. Reverb sends


3. Stereo width


4. Filter breathing


5. Micro-pitch drift




---

Do not do

No obvious beat

No dramatic bass drop

No lush over-arranging

No “final resolution”

No sharp attack at the ending



---

Arc in one line

Occupied room → dual relation → held breath → structural standing → harmonic bloom → human return → unresolved completion → brief turn → warm settlement
